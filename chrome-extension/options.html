Usage Guide

1. Login to seller.indiamart.com
2. Click the LeadMed extension icon
3. Click ▶ Start Capture
4. The extension will automatically extract your session cookies and capture leads
5. Leads appear in your LeadMed dashboard in real-time
6. High-priority leads trigger browser notifications
